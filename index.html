About Me

I am a recent graduate from Texas State University, holding a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a minor in Business Administration. As a passionate technology enthusiast, I have deepened my understanding of complex systems and honed my problem-solving skills throughout my academic journey. This has fueled my interest in IT, Data Analytics, and Cybersecurity. Through hands-on projects and coursework, I have developed a versatile skill set, positioning me well for a variety of tech roles.

In the field of IT, I have gained practical experience in system administration, network management, and technical support. My work with Raspberry Pi devices has allowed me to integrate specialized functionalities ranging from home automation to network-level content filtering and security enhancements. These projects reinforced the importance of clear communication, thorough documentation, and a user-focused approach. In addition, I have engaged with Python-based frameworks—such as Flask—to develop and deploy tailored web applications, refining both the technical architecture and the user experience. Involvement in extracurricular activities like Boko Hacks, the Google Developer Student Club, and IEEE has further broadened my professional network and exposed me to collaborative problem-solving approaches.

In the realm of Data Analytics, I have honed my skills in data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, and statistical modeling. Data Analytics and Cybersecurity are areas where I am particularly proficient. I have a strong command of SQL and MySQL, having managed large datasets to extract actionable insights, and I am well-versed in statistical methods and probability distributions, essential for data-driven decision-making. My Cybersecurity experience includes working with proactive security measures, network traffic analysis, and system exploitation mitigation techniques. Staying updated with industry trends and emerging technologies ensures that I can effectively protect digital assets while also implementing robust security protocols.

As I embark on my professional journey, I remain committed to continuous learning and excellence, eager to apply my comprehensive skill set and deep knowledge to make a meaningful impact in IT, Data Analytics, or Cybersecurity.
